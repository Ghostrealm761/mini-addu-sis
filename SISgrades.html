Grade Encoding Guidelines

  1. Grades in the New Grading System must be encoded as A, B+, B, C+, C, D, F, FD, WP
  2. Non FYDP Grades in the OLD Grading System must be from 65 to 100
  3. FYDP Grades in the NEW Grading System must be encoded as A, B+, B, C+, C, D, F, FD, WP
  4. FYDP Grades in the OLD Grading System must be encoded as 0- Outsanding, HS - Highly Satisfactory, S - Satisfactory, MS - Moderately Satisfactory, F- Fair, P - Poor
  5. NE Grades will not be accepted, Students who have not taken exams must be provided the minimum grade. After the exam has been taken, the teacher should file a grade correction form at the Registrar's Office
  6. Select AUD for Audit students.
